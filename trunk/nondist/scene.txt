Bad Lands - A portion of the Nevada desert that has been turned into a deadly wasteland. Was home to government experiments in time travel, until the experiment exploded. End result is a highly unstable, highly dangerous hole in time. Just being close to the bad lands can knock years off your life. Trying to enter the whole will kill you instantly.

Base - The base of operations for Tech and Ghost. It's an old somewhat leaky bunker near the Badlands that Tech used to build his time travel equipment in.

Time Keepers Base - High tech facility where the time keepers are stationed. Houses their Armory, Barracks, Jail, Computer Center, R&D and Medical facilities.
